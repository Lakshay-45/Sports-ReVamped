Prize Distribution System:



  • This web app uses centralised points distribution system, under which all the prizes are distributed according to set norms always.

  • Under the pre-defined system, it is the admin's sole duty to make sure all the prizes are distributed fairly, and under admin's own discretion, this duty can be assigned to anyone of his/her choosing.

  • It will also be admin's duty to ensure that the prizes are distributed before a tournament that has been completed is taken down from the web app.

  • For any institute seeking to either create a tournament or take part in a tournament, it is necessary to create an account on this web app, if not done already, so that points and awards can be distributed to the said institute by the organisers as well as the admin.

  • Along with the prize money decided by the organisers of the tournament, this website will also award points to each institute that has participated in the said tournaments, to maintain overall and sport-specefic institute rankings.

  • Under the norms, all the awards wether monetary or in points on the website, will be distributed as follows:

  • First place: 50% of prize pool and 500 points on the web-app to the institute.

  • Second place: 30% of prize pool and 300 points on the web-app to the institute.

  • Third place: 20% of prize pool and 200 points on the web-app to the institute.

  • However, the distribution of the prize pool can be changed by the organisers of the tournament on their own discretion, but if wishing to make such changes, the organisers have to make sure the admin and all the participants of the said tournament are aware of this change before the start of the tournament.

  • Any others awards, such as medals, certificates, etc. can be distributed by the organisers on their own discretion and are not the duty of the admin to forsee.

  • Organisers also have to keep in mind that any tournament they create must be up on the web-app for atleast a period of 3 weeks before it starts so that all institutes have enough time for registration and preparation for the said tournament, and it is also the duty of admin to make sure this rule is strictly adhered to.

  • If you still have some doubts or questions, please contact the admin from the contact us link up top.
